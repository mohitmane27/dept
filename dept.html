Welcome to Our Department

The Department of Information Technology at Government College of Engineering, Aurangabad was established in 1999 and has been at the forefront of IT education and research for over two decades.

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive education in information technology theory and practice, preparing students for successful careers in the rapidly evolving tech industry.

Academic Programs

We offer the following programs:

  • B.Tech in Information Technology
  • M.Tech in Information Technology
  • Ph.D. in Information Technology

Research Areas

Our faculty and students conduct research in several cutting-edge areas including:

  • Cloud Computing and Virtualization
  • Data Science and Big Data Analytics
  • Web Technologies and Mobile Computing
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Network Security
